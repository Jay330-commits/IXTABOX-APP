## What Sites Like Shopify Do (Additional Recommendations)

### 1. Content Marketing & Blog
**Why**: Fresh, valuable content signals authority and gets backlinks
- Create a blog section (`/blog` or `/news`)
- Write articles about:
  - "How to Pack Your Car for a Road Trip"
  - "Roof Box vs Cargo Box: Which is Better?"
  - "Travel Tips for Camping with Extra Storage"
  - "IXTAbox Success Stories"
- Target long-tail keywords like "how to rent roof box Sweden"

### 2. Backlink Strategy
**Why**: High-quality backlinks = higher domain authority
- Partner with:
  - Travel blogs
  - Car/automotive websites
  - Camping and outdoor gear sites
  - Swedish/Nordic tourism sites
- Create shareable content (infographics, guides)
- Guest posting on relevant blogs
- Press releases for new locations/features

### 3. Local SEO Optimization
**Why**: You serve specific locations (Sweden, Nordics)
- Create location-specific pages:
  - `/locations/stockholm`
  - `/locations/gothenburg`
  - `/locations/malmo`
- Add LocalBusiness schema to each location
- Get listed on Google Business Profile
- Add location-specific content and keywords

### 4. User-Generated Content
**Why**: Social proof and fresh content
- Customer reviews/testimonials with schema
- Photo galleries from customers
- Social media integration
- Review widgets on site

### 5. Technical SEO Enhancements
**Why**: Better crawling and indexing
- ✅ Already have: Sitemap, robots.txt, structured data
- Add: 
  - Page speed optimization (already good with Next.js)
  - Mobile-first indexing (already responsive)
  - HTTPS (ensure SSL certificate)
  - Core Web Vitals monitoring

### 6. Social Media Presence
**Why**: Social signals help SEO
- Active presence on:
  - Instagram (visual content)
  - Facebook (community)
  - LinkedIn (B2B partnerships)
  - YouTube (tutorial videos)
- Share content regularly
- Link back to website

### 7. Email Marketing & Retention
**Why**: Returning visitors = better metrics
- Newsletter with travel tips
- Booking reminders
- Post-rental follow-ups
- Special offers for repeat customers

### 8. Partnerships & Collaborations
**Why**: Cross-promotion and backlinks
- Partner with:
  - Car rental companies
  - Hotels and accommodations
  - Tourism boards
  - Outdoor gear retailers
- Create co-marketing campaigns
- Exchange links/mentions

### 9. Performance & User Experience
**Why**: Google ranks fast, user-friendly sites higher
- ✅ Already optimized with Next.js
- Monitor Core Web Vitals
- A/B test conversion elements
- Improve mobile experience

### 10. Google Search Console & Analytics
**Why**: Data-driven optimization
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Monitor search performance
- Fix crawl errors
- Track keyword rankings
- Analyze user behavior

- **Organic traffic**: Google Analytics

- **Backlinks**: Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Moz
- **Domain Authority**: Moz Domain Authority
- **Search impressions**: Google Search Console
- **Click-through rate**: Google Search Console
